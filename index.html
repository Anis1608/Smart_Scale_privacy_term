Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 08, 2026

At Smart Scale, we prioritize your data security and transparency.

1. Data Collection

Our application collects weight readings streamed from your hardware device to provide a localized and cloud-synced history.

  • Measurement Data: Weight values, stability status, and unit types.
  • Connectivity Info: Device IDs for Bluetooth and WiFi management.

2. Location & Bluetooth Permissions

To facilitate connections with your IoT hardware, Android and iOS require Bluetooth and Location permissions. Note: We do not track or store your geographical location.

3. Data Sync

Data is transmitted via a secure Socket.io connection to our production server (43.205.128.159) to allow multi-device monitoring.

4. Security

We implement industry-standard encryption to ensure your weighing logs are not accessible to unauthorized third parties.
